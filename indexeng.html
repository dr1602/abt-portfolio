About iHealth, now Florence Nightingale

We make things easier when you need it the most.

We digitize complementary activities to medical services so that you can focus on getting well.

Consultation

We digitize your medical history and required documents so that you can:

  • 1. Have Medical Portability.
  • 2. Enjoy your time by having your documents at hand.
  • 3. Facilitate your care and follow-up by having your medical records digitized.

Medical Insurance

With AI assistance, we provide you with the best medical insurance recommendations based on your digital history.

In addition to that, with your digital history and documents, we facilitate and guide you to obtain the best insurance according to your needs.

Preoperative

Florence Nightingale is your virtual advisor for managing preoperative requirements, both administrative and therapeutic or medical.

With AI assistance, we recommend the best laboratories available to you based on your needs.

Finances

Whether you have medical insurance or not, we help you manage your finances with tools such as:

  • 1. Budgets.
  • 2. Payment agreements (time and type of payment).
  • 3. Expense vs Budget Comparison.
  • 4. Financing alternatives.

Postoperative

Florence Nightingale is your virtual advisor for managing postoperative processes, including rehabilitation recommendations and claims closure for medical insurance.
